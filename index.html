Hi there, I'm Carolina 

For most of my life I felt not enough

Not pretty enough

Not slim enough

Not attractive enough

Just not good enough

I battled anxiety and eating disorders for many, many years. I would punish my body for not being like the ones in the magazines. I would workout for hours and eat next to nothing all day only to feel in control of myself. Through this unhealthy process I got to weigh 45 kilos...but I still wasn’t happy.

As I became thinner than ever and I was still feeling like sh*t, I thought "well, maybe i should just quit trying to fit the norm and just relax". I timidly started the process of learning to love myself by studying everything about nutrition, meditation and holistic wellness and I took a vow of sharing my lessons with other women along the journey.

For the next 10 years I tried to live a "normal" life. I moved countries and I got married to an amazing man, started a successful business in the fitness industry and created a beautiful community of friends: "My life is perfect, I finally love myself!" 

I didn't know yet, but my self-love was still built on external factors...and a series of events came to shake it all up! Out of fear, disconnection to myself, lack of self worth and self-awareness I decided to put my life on hold and myself in the back burner to follow the man to a different country (because that’s what a good wife’s supposed to do!, no?), despite knowing that deep down, I wasn’t listening to my truth. In the process I gave up my business, my financial independence, and my self-confidence. I spiralled into a horrible depression, started suffering severe anxiety, gained 12 kilos through emotional eating, and felt totally miserable for many, many months.

Enter Bali... 

In an attempt to get my head out of the waters and following my passion for Yoga I enrolled in a Teacher Training that would catalyse an awakening process. After several months of back and forth, I took the heartbreaking decision to end my marriage and leave the comfort and familiarity of a 10+ years relationship that wasn’t in alignment with my path anymore. I needed to know who I was and what was my mission in this life.

That’s when my self love journey truly started: when I devotedly committed to explore and hold space for all parts of myself, specially the not so pretty ones. I lifted the carpet where I'd hidden my BS for decades and oh darling, there was a lot of it! Unhealthy patterns, old trauma, victim stories, co-dependancy and a not-so-protective-anymore perfectionism.

I spent the next couple of years attending workshops, retreats and personal development courses, studying, researching and breathing everyday into my quest of becoming the woman I want to be. I hired coaches and mentors and continued teaching along the way as I learnt more and more tools about self love.

Today I can look myself in the mirror and honestly say: "I love myself". I learnt to love my wrinkles, my cellulite, my jiggly bits and my chunky legs. I learnt to accept all my emotions (not just the "good" ones), to speak my truth even if my voice shakes, to be ok with not having my sh*t together all the time. I learnt to fill my own cup first, to draw healthy boundaries and say no when I need to without guilt or shame. I learnt that forgiveness and compassion towards myself and others, is way better than living in paralyzing and soul-destroying perfectionism as I used to.

The change hasn't been easy but it started with the decision to create a life that I actually loved...and i believe that loving ourselves is our birthright!

Since 2009, as a qualified Personal Trainer, Pilates, Dance, Yoga & Meditation facilitator, and Women's Coach, I've helped thousand of women to become fitter and healthier inside and out, own their worth, build up their self esteem and self confidence.

My passion in life is to support women to feel EPIC, BEAUTIFUL, HEALTHY, SEXY and HAPPY…. to love themselves FULLY no matter what!

My biggest dream is to live in a world where we all feel empowered, worthy, confident and walking our truth by taking care of our bodies, minds and hearts everyday.

In a society that profits from our self-doubt and controls us with incessant messages about how we need to be different than who we are, I firmly believe we can change the world by simply being a woman in love with her whole Self.
